About Me

As a fervent educator, visionary academic leader, and seasoned expert in HR and operations, I bring over 23 years of multifaceted experience spanning India and the Middle East to every endeavor. My professional trajectory encompasses classroom instruction and curriculum innovation, as well as strategic leadership in academic administration and human resource management. I have refined my expertise by teaching Business Studies, Economics, Accounting, and Computer Studies within both IGCSE and CBSE frameworks. My creative and adaptable pedagogical approach consistently motivates students to engage in critical thinking and achieve academic excellence. In leadership capacities—as Vice Principal, Head of Department, and in executive roles—I have pioneered initiatives that seamlessly integrate academic rigor with progressive administrative practices, ensuring that educational institutions prosper in an ever-evolving landscape. My academic credentials, which include a Ph.D. in Management, an MBA in HR & Marketing, and a range of specialized certifications, attest to my unwavering commitment to continuous learning and professional excellence. I have adeptly formulated and implemented strategic initiatives in HR, admissions, branding, and digital marketing, always prioritizing the cultivation of a positive and dynamic environment for both educators and students. Whether I am designing avant-garde curricula, mentoring faculty, or steering comprehensive HR operations for large-scale organizations, I am propelled by a singular vision: to empower individuals to realize their full potential through superior education and innovative leadership. I eagerly anticipate connecting with fellow professionals and institutions that share my dedication to educational and organizational excellence..
